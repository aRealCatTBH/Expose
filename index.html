Your Discord Rich Presence Has Been revolutionized.
Expose is a browser extension that connects to Discord's local RPC server, and shows what tab you have open.

It also can connect to arRPC, An open implementation of Discord's local RPC servers.

arRPC allows RPC for setups that use Discord Web or custom clients.
Expose was inspiered by PreMid and is just a PoC (proof of concept)

It's up to you to pick which you use
